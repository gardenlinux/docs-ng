# Tutorials

Tutorials are **learning-oriented** lessons that take you through a series of steps to complete a project. They are designed to help you learn by doing, providing a safe environment to get started with Garden Linux.

<SectionIndex />

---

**About Tutorials**: Following the [Diátaxis framework](https://diataxis.fr/), tutorials focus on practical education. They guide you through a complete project, teaching you the fundamentals while building something functional. Tutorials are perfect for newcomers who want hands-on experience with Garden Linux.
